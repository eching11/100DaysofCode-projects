It's Pomodoro time!

The Pomodoro Technique is a widely used time management tool. Get things done faster when you work in spurts.

  1. Complete an uninterrupted 25 minute working period.
  2. Then, take a quick 5 minute break.
  3. Repeat steps 1 & 2 for 4 times total.
  4. Finally, after all that productivity, take a long break of 15-30 minutes.
